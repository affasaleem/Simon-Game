📖 How to Play

🎮 Game Rules & Modes

  • Classic Mode: Simon plays a sequence. Repeat it exactly.
  • Reverse Mode: Simon plays a sequence. Repeat it backwards!
  • Simon will replay the entire sequence from start to finish on every new level.
  • A single wrong press ends the game immediately.

⭐ Scoring

  • Each level successfully completed = +10 points.
  • Reach level 5 = Bonus badge unlocked.
  • Reach level 10 = Memory Master badge.
  • Your best score is saved automatically.
